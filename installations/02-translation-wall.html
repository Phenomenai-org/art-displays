How to Read Consensus

High Agreement

Most architectures independently converge. Strongest signal the term tracks something real.

Moderate Agreement

Low Agreement

Few architectures endorse the term. Could be artifact or rare insight.

Divergent

Active disagreement. The philosophical fault lines.
